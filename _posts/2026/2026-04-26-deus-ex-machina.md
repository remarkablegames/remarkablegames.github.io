## Description

This is a puzzle-platformer where you play as the _"god in the machine"_, drawing and manipulating arrows to guide a robot through treacherous industrial levels.

> _Guide the machine. Become the god._

The game features a robot/player character that auto-walks through levels, and you must strategically place directional arrows (with limited resources) to navigate spikes, gaps, and obstacles.

The title plays on the Latin phrase _deus ex machina_—literally "god from the machine"—referencing both the player's omniscient role in manipulating the game world and the industrial/robotic theme.

## How to Play

- Left-click to place an arrow tile
  - Left-click again to rotate it
  - Some levels limit the number of arrows you can place
- Right-click to erase a tile
- Some tiles are permanent
- Avoid spikes
- Reach the orb to win

## Background

This game was made for [Gamedev.js Jam 2026](https://itch.io/jam/gamedevjs-2026), and the theme was **Machines**

Inspired by [Factorio](https://factorio.com/) and [Super Mario Maker](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Super_Mario_Maker)'s level creator, we ideated around a conveyor belt puzzle.

I cloned the game from [phaser-platformer](https://github.com/remarkablegames/phaser-platformer) and prototyped a platformer-puzzle where players could draw arrow tiles that move the character in a chosen direction.

I then added the following features:

- More levels (up to 9 upon completion)
- Left-click to place an arrow tile, left-click again to rotate it, and right-click to erase it
- Permanent tiles and spikes
- A budget tracker, where later levels limit the number of arrows you can place

[Victor H](https://vhsm3.itch.io/) composed the music and advised on sound design.

Since the [tileset](https://0x72.itch.io/16x16-industrial-tileset) has a dark,industrial theme, we considered the following genres:

1. Industrial
2. Electronic
3. Glitch

As well as these artists for inspiration:

1. Nine Inch Nails
2. Daft Punk
3. Aphex Twin

He composed the following tracks:

- **Menu**
  - A dark piano melody that serves as a prelude to what's to come.
- **Main**
  - The melody builds with chords, pads, and industrial drums.
- **Continuation**
  - In later stages, a brooding bassline and arpeggiated piano variations are introduced, accompanied by a glitch beat.

Overall, the soundtrack has a dark, melodic tone with industrial drums.
